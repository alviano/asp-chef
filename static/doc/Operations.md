The **Operations** operation does nothing... but can be used to add other ingredients above it!

§§§§

This operation is a specialized UI helper designed to facilitate the rapid building of recipes.

#### How it works

When you add an **Operations** ingredient to your recipe, it displays a searchable list of all available operations directly within the recipe panel. Clicking on any operation in this list will insert a new ingredient **above** the **Operations** ingredient.

#### Use Case: Recipe Entry Point

Place an **Operations** ingredient at the end of your recipe or at a specific insertion point. Instead of constantly moving back and forth between the sidebar and your recipe, you can use this "anchor" to quickly append new processing steps or visualizations at that exact location.

#### Features

*   **Integrated Search**: Quickly find the operation you need without leaving the recipe flow.
*   **Sequential Insertion**: Each new ingredient added via this tool is placed immediately preceding it, keeping your workflow centered.
*   **Nop-like Behavior**: Like the **Nop** operation, it does not modify the models passing through the recipe.
